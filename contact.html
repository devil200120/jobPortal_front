Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you find candidates for my open position?

Our typical turnaround time is 48-72 hours for initial candidate presentations. For urgent positions, we can often provide qualified candidates within 24 hours.

What industries do you specialize in?

We serve 8 major industries including Healthcare, IT & Technology, Industrial, Cleaning Services, Administrative, Education, Hospitality, and Retail.

Is there a fee for job seekers to use your services?

No, our services are completely free for job seekers. We are paid by employers when we successfully place candidates.

How do you screen candidates?

We use a comprehensive screening process including skills assessments, background checks, reference verification, and in-depth interviews to ensure quality matches.
